$elemMatch 用于在数组类型的字段中选择元素, 需要数组至少有一个元素符合多个查询条件时, $elemMatch 尤其有用:

1
2

// the restaurants that achieved a score, more than 80 but less than 90
db.restaurants.find({grades: {$elemMatch: {score: {$gt: 80, $lt:90}} }})
